Complete!

Tuesday, December 02, 2014
Posted in ruby, rails, study, WGR, f2yd

The app is complete! I think.

Everything we needed in order to get the ball rolling is in place. I had to contract out the last 5% that I was stuck on, but soon we'll be accepting customers at Forms to Your Door. We have a few UI tweaks left to make and some rewording of the copy is necessary, but this thing is ready to go! I'm excited. We put a lot of time (and some money) into this idea and I have learned a significant amount about Ruby, Rails, and what it takes to build a web app. I have a lot to learn still, I know.

At this moment, I have taken a step back and am learning Ruby from the ground up with The Well-Grounded Rubyist. Next up I'll be working through the book Justin Weiss is writing, Practicing Rails.
